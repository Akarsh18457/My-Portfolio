### Key Findings:
1.  **Demographics:** Older customers (40-60 age group) are significantly more likely to churn.
2.  **Geography:** 'Germany' has the highest churn rate and should be a priority for regional retention campaigns.
3.  **Financials:** High-balance customers are leaving at a higher rate than low-balance customers, posing a severe revenue risk.
